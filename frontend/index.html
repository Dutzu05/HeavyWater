Preview configuration summary

Water Bodies OpenStreetMap Overpass

Controls the source of hydrological data. Options include live data from 'OpenStreetMap Overpass' or local high-resolution datasets like 'Local EuHydro'.

Communities Raster Not set

Points to a local GeoTIFF file representing populated areas. If provided, the generator overlays these boundaries directly onto the preview map.

Threshold 1

Determines the pixel intensity threshold for community extraction. Higher values filter out more noise, keeping only densely populated areas.

Minimum Area 2000 m2

Sets the minimum footprint for a community polygon. Any generated shape smaller than this area will be ignored, preventing tiny, irrelevant clusters.

Step 1 - Target Location Latitude, Longitude, AOI size

Pick the project point by clicking on the map or typing coordinates. AOI size defines how wide the analysis area is around that point.

Step 2 - Community Inputs Raster, Threshold, Min Area, Merge

These settings shape how settlement polygons are extracted and cleaned: threshold filters noise, minimum area removes tiny fragments, and merge distance joins nearby pieces.

Step 3 - Terrain Overlay Terrain toggle and resolution

Enable this to include elevation-driven context. Resolution sets the detail level of terrain sampling, balancing precision and runtime.

Step 4 - Hydrology and Metrics River metrics, discharge, lookback

River metrics adds water indicators, while discharge simulation adds modeled flow behavior. Resolution and lookback define spatial detail and historical window.

Step 5 - Ground Stability EGMS motion screening

Stability analysis checks ground movement trends. Buffer controls the search radius around your AOI and motion threshold sets the sensitivity for flagged movement.

Step 6 - Water Risk Logic Community or farm-point risk mode

Choose whether risk is scored for all communities or a specific farm point. Farm demand and GloFAS history tune the demand pressure and flow-history context.

Step 7 - Run and Review Generate View, Reset, Preview tab

Generate View runs the selected pipeline. Reset restores defaults. Results appear in the Preview tab and linked reports update when available.
